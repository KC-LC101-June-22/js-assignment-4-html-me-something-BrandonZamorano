My Hobbies and Intests:

People may have a variety of different hobbies. Some like to run while others my prefer going out for a walk. Others may like to draw, while others may like to read or write. While my hobbies may not be as exciting as snowboarding or parachuting, I will tell you about a few of my hobbies and interests.

  • Learning
  • Programming
  • Video Games

Learning

Learning is something that I think everyone enjoys.I believe that everyone likes to improve themselves and get better at something and that goes for me as well. I enjoy learning. I enjoy the process of making mistakes, doing the work to improve, and especially those moments when things just click and you understand something that you had previosuly struggled with. I imagine weight lifters enjoy a similar feeling when they reach a new personal best. That being said, I don't enjoy learning just anything without a reason or interest.

That's how I felt about learning math during high school. I didn't really know or have a way to apply my math skills then, but now I do after decided to pursue programming. Needless to say, now that I've seen interesting applications that require math, I've decided to relearn what I forgot and progress my math skills.

Programming

The thought of being able to create things with just a computer was what caught my interest. I already had a computer and access to a variety of resources thanks to the internet. I didn't have to buy anything else, so I figured I might as well try it out.

After writing my first "Hello world!" program, I was hooked. I could tell the computer to do what I need it to do. The only thing limiting me was my knowledge. In other words, I think learning about programming, fueled my interest in learning in general.
